Cajun Pasta

Food | Pasta | Cajun

Now I love any dish with heavy cream and my protein of choice. But Cajun Pasta really takes the cake! A fusion dish at it's core, it has the perfect blend of Italian, French, and Lousiana all on one plate. If you love the creaminess of a chicken alfredo and the spiceness of Lousiana seasoning...you won't regret trying this dish.
